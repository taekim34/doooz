## Day 1: Sign Up and Create Your Family

### A Parent Signs Up First

DOOOZ is designed so that a parent creates the family, and kids join using an invite code. It all starts when the first parent signs up with their email.

1. When you open the app, you'll see the login screen. Tap **"Sign Up"**.
2. Enter your email and a password (6+ characters), then tap the **"Create Family"** button.
3. A verification email will arrive. Open your inbox and click the link to complete verification!
4. Go back to the login screen and log in with your email and password.

> **Tip:** If you don't see the verification email, check your spam folder.

---

### Create Your Family

After logging in, you'll be taken straight to the family creation screen.

1. Choose a **family name**. (e.g., "The Smiths", "Happy House")
2. Enter **your name**. (e.g., "Mom", "Dad", "Sarah's Mom")
3. Set an **invite code** yourself, or leave it blank to auto-generate one. (e.g., "ourfamily")
4. Select your **timezone**. If you're in Korea, Asia/Seoul is the default.
5. Select a **language**. You can choose from 한국어, English, or 日本語.
6. Tap the **"Create Family"** button, and you'll move on to the character selection screen.
7. Pick a character you like! There are 12 to choose from!

> **Note:** The family name acts as a login ID, so it must be unique across all families. You can always check the invite code later under the Family tab → Invite.

---

### Invite Kids and Other Family Members

Once the family is created, it's time to invite kids or another parent. Kids can sign up without an email!

1. Open the app on the device of the person being invited.
2. On the login screen, tap the **"Join with Invite Code (No Email Required)"** link.
3. Enter the **family name**. (Ask the parent who created the family)
4. Enter the **invite code**.
5. Choose a **name**. (e.g., "Alex", "Emma")
6. Set a **password** (6+ characters).
7. Select a **role** — "Child" or "Parent". Available features differ by role. Parents can create/manage tasks, pardon, and manage rewards. Kids can check off tasks, beg, request rewards, and change characters.
8. Tap the **"Join Family"** button, pick a character, and you're done!

> **How to log in:** All family members log in via the "Family Name" tab using **family name + name + password**. The first parent can also log in via the "Email" tab using email + password.

## Day 1: Parents Create Tasks

Now that the kids have joined, let's create some tasks!

1. Tap the **"Tasks"** tab in the bottom navigation.
2. Tap the **"Manage"** link in the top right.
3. Tap the **"Add Task"** button.

### Creating a Recurring Task (Weekly Repeats)

4. Enter a **title**. (e.g., "Read a book", "Clean your room")
5. Select the **assigned child**.
6. Set the **points** — choose from Easy (50pt), Normal (100pt), Hard (150pt), or enter a custom amount with "Custom".
7. Select the **repeat days**. (e.g., Mon–Fri only, or every day)
8. Set the **start date**.
9. Optionally set an **end date**. (Optional)
10. Tap **"Save"** and you're done!

### Creating a One-Time Goal

4. Enter a title. (e.g., "Write a book report")
5. Select the assigned child.
6. Set the points.
7. Set the repeat to **"Once"**.
8. Set the **due date**. (Complete it by this day)
9. Tap **"Save"** and you're done!

> **Tip:** You can add tasks for today, but you can also set long-term challenge goals with big point rewards. For example, setting "Build an app with AI vibe coding (1000pt)" means your child can also earn the **Big Goal Achieved** badge when they complete it!

> **Edit/Delete:** On the manage screen, you can edit any existing task's title, points, assignee, start date, and end date. To change the repeat setting, you'll need to delete the task and create a new one.
> 
> **Ended Tasks:** Tasks past their end date appear in an "Ended" section. If you no longer need them, use "Permanently Delete" to remove them for good.

## Day 2: Kids Check Off Tasks

### The Kid's Home Screen

When a kid logs in, their home screen shows:

- Their **character** and **level progress bar** (current points / points needed for next level)
- **Current point balance** on the right
- **Today's tasks** list
- **Badges** earned so far

### Checking Off Tasks

1. Tap the **"Tasks"** tab in the bottom navigation.
2. You'll see today's task list. **Check off** the ones you've completed!
3. Points are earned instantly when you check! (You'll see a message like "+100pt!" along with your balance)

> **Undo warning:** If you checked by mistake, you can tap again to undo — but your points will be taken back, and any badges earned in the meantime might be revoked!

### Upcoming Tasks

If you have one-time goals due after today, they'll appear in the "Upcoming Tasks" section with countdowns like **D-3**, **D-7**. Check ahead and get prepared.

### Missed Tasks

Tasks you didn't finish by yesterday appear in a red "Missed Tasks" section. (An automatic -50pt penalty has already been applied at midnight.)

### Want to Do More? Beg!

Did something that's not on your task list and want points for it? Try the "Beg" feature.

1. You'll find the **"Beg"** section below the Tasks tab.
2. Write what you did. (e.g., "Helped with the dishes")
3. Tap the **"Request"** button, and your parents will be notified.
4. If your parents approve, you'll earn points!

> **Beg status:** After requesting, it shows up in your "Pending" list. Changed your mind? You can cancel it yourself. Parents can also reject it.

## Day 2: Parents Review

### The Parent's Home Screen

When a parent logs in, the home screen shows each kid's status in card form:

- Each child's **character**, **level**, **point balance**
- Today's task **progress** (completed / total + progress bar)
- Completion status of each task

> **Detail view:** Tap a child's card to go to their detail page. You can see stats, today's tasks, badges, and recent point history at a glance.

### Reviewing Beg Requests

When a kid sends a Beg request, you'll get a push notification, and a yellow "Beg Requests" section appears in the Tasks tab.

1. Check the **"Beg Requests"** in the Tasks tab.
2. See the child's name and request, then tap **"Approve"** or **"Reject"**.
3. When approving, you can set the point amount yourself!

### Pardon

Did your child miss a task but had a good reason? Use "Pardon" to waive the penalty.

1. In the Tasks tab, there's a **"Pardon"** button next to your child's incomplete or overdue tasks.
2. Tap it, and the task changes to "Pardoned" status.
3. If the -50pt penalty was already deducted, an automatic +50pt credit is applied.
4. If the penalty hasn't been deducted yet, it prevents future deduction.

> **Undo Pardon:** If you pardoned by mistake, you can undo it.

## Day 3: What Happens at Night?

DOOOZ automatically does two things every day:

### 1:00 AM (Automatic Processing)

- Tasks not completed yesterday change to **"Overdue"** status, and **-50pt** is deducted per task.
- Recurring tasks for today's date are **automatically generated**.

### 9:00 PM (Reminder)

- Kids with unfinished tasks receive a **push notification**.
- Something like: "You still have tasks left today! 3 remaining: Read a book, Clean your room, Write in diary"

> **Tip:** Check off your tasks before 1:00 AM to avoid the penalty! Pay attention to the 9 PM reminder.

## Day 4–5: Points Mean Rewards!

### Parents: Create Rewards

As kids complete tasks, they'll accumulate points. Set up rewards they can exchange points for!

1. Tap the **"Rewards"** tab in the bottom navigation. (Parents are automatically taken to the reward management screen)
2. Under **"New Reward"**, enter a reward name. (e.g., "Ice cream", "Movie night")
3. Set the **required points**. (e.g., 500)
4. Tap **"Add"** and it's registered!

> **Delete:** You can delete rewards you no longer want to offer.

### Kids: Request Rewards

1. Tap the **"Rewards"** tab in the bottom navigation.
2. You'll see the list of available rewards with their point costs.
3. Your current point balance is shown in the top right.
4. Tap the **"I Want This!"** button on the reward you want.
5. Once your parent approves, points are automatically deducted. Actual rewards (allowance, special activities, etc.) are given offline — the app doesn't track delivery separately.

> **Cancel request:** Changed your mind? You can cancel a pending request.
> 
> **Rejected?** If a parent rejects your request, a reason is shown. No points are deducted.
> 
> **My request status:** At the bottom of the Rewards tab, you can see all your requests — pending, approved, rejected, and canceled.

## Day 7: Level Up and Badges!

### Level System (30 Levels)

As points accumulate, your level goes up automatically! You might see a "Level Up!" message each time you complete a task.

- Lv.1 Sprout → Lv.5 Tree → Lv.10 Meteor → Lv.15 Eternal → Lv.20 Immortal → Lv.25 Eon → Lv.30 Transcendent
- 30 levels total, automatically calculated based on cumulative points!

### Badge System (58 Types)

Earn badges automatically by meeting various conditions. Check your earned badges on the home screen and character page.

> **Badges can be revoked:** If you undo a task check, you may no longer meet the conditions, and the badge could disappear. Be careful!

### Character Evolution (5 Stages)

Your chosen character evolves through 5 stages based on your level:

- **Hatchling** → **Rookie** → **Warrior** → **Hero** → **Legend**

### Changing Characters

1. Tap the **"Character"** tab in the bottom navigation.
2. In the **"Character Gallery"**, tap an unlocked character to switch.

> **Note:** Only kids can change characters directly from the Character tab. Parents access the character change link from Settings.

## Other Useful Features

### Review Past Tasks

Want to see what was done in the past?

1. Tap the **"History"** link in the **"Tasks"** tab.
2. Use the "← Previous Day" / "Next Day →" buttons to browse by date.
3. Parents can filter by child, and change completion status or pardon past tasks.
4. Kids can view history in read-only mode.

> **Parent tip:** "You can undo or re-complete tasks here if a child checked something incorrectly or a fix is needed."

### Check Points

1. Tap the **"Points"** tab in the bottom navigation.
2. See your **current balance** and **total earned points** at a glance.
3. Parents see each child's balance and recent transaction history.
4. In **"Point History"**, view all earned/deducted entries in chronological order. (Loads 50 at a time with "Load More")

### Explore the Family

1. Tap the **"Family"** tab in the bottom navigation.
2. See a list of all family members.
3. Tap a member to go to their detailed profile page:
   - Character, level, stage progress
   - Activity days, join date, and other stats
   - Today's task status
   - Earned badges
   - Recent point history

> **Check invite code:** Go to Family tab → "Invite" to view and copy the invite code. Use it to add new family members.

### Change Settings

Tap the **"Settings"** tab in the bottom navigation:

- **Change Name** — Update your display name.
- **Change Password** — Enter your current password and set a new one (6+ characters).
- **Change Character** — Go to the gallery screen.
- **Family Settings** (Parents only) — Change family name, language (한국어/English/日本語), and timezone.
- **Log Out**

### Install as an App (PWA)

DOOOZ works right in your browser without installing anything, but **adding it to your home screen gives you the full experience as a standalone app with all features!**

**Android:**

1. Open DOOOZ in Chrome.
2. Tap the top-right menu (three dots) → **"Add to Home Screen"** → **"Install app"**.

**iPhone:**

1. Open DOOOZ in Safari. (Must be Safari!)
2. Tap the share button at the bottom (square with arrow) → **"Add to Home Screen"**. Make sure "Open as Web App" is enabled, then tap **"Add"**.

> **Enable notifications:** After installing, allow notification permissions. This lets you receive push notifications for Beg requests, rewards, reminders, and more.

### Push Notification Types

Here are the push notifications DOOOZ sends:

| Situation              | Who receives it? | Content                                                     |
| ---------------------- | ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Beg request            | Parent           | "Alex is asking you to recognize 'Helped with the dishes'!" |
| Beg approved           | Child            | "'Helped with the dishes' approved! +100pt"                 |
| Beg rejected           | Child            | "'Helped with the dishes' request was rejected"             |
| Beg canceled           | Parent           | "Alex canceled their request"                               |
| Reward approved        | Child            | "'Ice cream' reward has been approved!"                     |
| Reward rejected        | Child            | "'Ice cream' reward has been rejected"                      |
| Evening reminder (9PM) | Child            | "You still have tasks left today! 3 remaining: ..."         |

> **Note:** Task check/uncheck notifications are excluded from push notifications because they happen too frequently and would be overwhelming.
